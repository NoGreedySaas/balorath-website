‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Balorath really free?

Yes! There are no subscriptions, no premium tiers, and no ads. Every feature is available from day one.

Where is my data stored?

All your workout data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data syncs across your Apple devices. We don't have access to your data.

How do I sync with Apple Health?

When you finish your first workout, you'll be prompted to enable Apple Health integration. You can also enable this anytime from Settings ‚Üí Apple Health.

Can I create my own exercises?

Absolutely! Go to the Exercises tab and tap the + button to add custom exercises with your preferred settings.

How do I customize my gym's equipment?

Go to Profile ‚Üí Gym Settings. You can set your barbell weights, available plates, and dumbbell/machine increments to match your gym exactly.

I accidentally deleted a workout. Can I recover it?

Unfortunately, deleted workouts cannot be recovered. If you have iCloud sync enabled, your data exists on your other devices until they sync.
